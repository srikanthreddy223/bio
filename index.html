PAVAN KALYAN

Pawan Kalyan (born Konidela Kalyan Babu; 2 September 1968 or 1971) is an Indian politician, actor, filmmaker, and philanthropist. He works in Telugu cinema and is the recipient of a Filmfare Award, a SIIMA Award, a CineMAA Award and a Santosham Film Award.

2013 Winner Filmfare Award - Telugu Film Industry. Best Actor. Gabbar Singh. 2009 winner Filmfare Award - Telugu Film Industry. Best Actor. Jalsa. 2002 winner Filmfare Award - Telugu Film Industry. Best Actor. Khushi.
